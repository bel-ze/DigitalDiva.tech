About Us

Promote Education and Attendance of Students in Classes

In present academic system, Zoom class attendance of students' plays a significant role in performance assessment and quality monitoring. The conventional methods practiced in most of the institutions are by calling names or signing on papers, which is highly time-consuming and insecure. Therefore we presents DigitalDive - the automatic attendance management system for attendande as well as studying encouragement, convenience and data reliability. The system is developed by the integration of ubiquitous components to make a portable device for managing the students' attendance using Face Recognition technology.

  • Promote Class Attendance
  • Save time to record attendance in classes
  • Encourage commitment in studying of students
